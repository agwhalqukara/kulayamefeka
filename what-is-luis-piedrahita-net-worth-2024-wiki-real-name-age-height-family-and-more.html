Early Beginnings and Magic Mastery

Luis Piedrahita, born on 19 February 1977 in A Coruña, Spain, embarked on his magical journey at a young age.

His fascination with magic wasn’t just a fleeting childhood interest; it was the beginning of what would become a distinguished career.

Luis’s talent was undeniable, earning him second place at the MagicValongo contest in Portugal in 1998, a precursor to his triumphant win as the Spanish Magic Champion the following year.

This early success laid the groundwork for his career, showcasing his dedication and skill in the art of magic.

Transition to Television and Comedy

However, his ambitions extended beyond the magician’s stage. His academic pursuits in Audiovisual Communications at the University of Navarra honed his skills in storytelling and scriptwriting, talents that would prove invaluable in his next career phase.
