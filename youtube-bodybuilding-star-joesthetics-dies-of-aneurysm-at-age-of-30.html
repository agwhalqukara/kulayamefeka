YouTube bodybuilding star, Joesthetics dies of 'aneurysm' at age of 30'

August 27, 2024
blog

The bodybuilding community has been shaken by the sudden passing of Jo Lindner, a well-known figure in the YouTube fitness world who was also known as “Joesthetics.” At just 30 years old, he tragically died of an aneurysm, leaving behind a grieving partner, Nicha, who shared the heartbreaking news on Instagram.

Nicha revealed that Jo had been experiencing neck pain in the days leading up to his passing, but they had not realized the severity of the situation until it was too late. She described him as an incredible person and shared touching photos of the couple during happier times.

Prior to his death, Jo had expressed concerns about over-training due to his rare muscular condition known as rippling muscle disease, which makes muscles overly sensitive to movement and pressure. In a previous interview, he had also voiced fears about the potential impact of the condition on his heart.

In the wake of Jo’s untimely death, Nicha paid tribute to him on social media, sharing cherished memories and asking others to remember him as Joesthetics. Additionally, his friend and fellow bodybuilder, Noel Deyzel, expressed his love for Jo and mourned his passing.

The bodybuilding community has lost a talented and passionate individual, and Jo Lindner’s legacy as Joesthetics will be remembered and cherished by many.
