Dancing With the Stars Pro Edyta Sliwinska Welcomes Baby No. 2

August 25, 2024
blog

Congratulations, Edyta Sliwinska!

The former Dancing With the Stars pro has a new reason to celebrate. The 36-year-old and her husband, Alec Mazo, welcomed their second child, daughter Leia Josephine, into the world on Sunday, June 18, at 7:08 p.m. The little bundle of joy weighed seven pounds and measured 20 inches long.

Fans were quick to comment on the photo to wish the dancer their congratulations. One fan wrote, "She is beautiful, just like mommy!" while another added, "If this isn't the cutest thing ever! She's smiling like 'I'm here mom and we're going to rule the world!'"
