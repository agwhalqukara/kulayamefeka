Career

Bill Nye started his career working at Boeing Corporation as an engineer which was located near Seattle. During this period he helped in transforming the Boeing 747 airplanes by developing a hydraulic suppressor tube. He aspired to become a NASA astronaut but was rejected.

In 1978, he won a ‘Steve Martin Look-Alike competition’ which motivated him to become a standup comedian. In 1993, he collaborated with KCTS-TV for broadcasting ‘Bill Nye the Science Guy.’

In 2013, he also participated in ‘Dancing with the Stars’ 17th Season but was eliminated after three episodes only. Bill Nye has also written books like ‘Unstoppable: Harnessing Science to Change the World’, and ‘Undeniable: Evolution and the Science of Creation.’ Bill Nye is also an environmental change activist who often blames humankind, for messing up with the science of mother earth.
